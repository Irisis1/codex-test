# Captions for Section 3.4 field-pattern figures

**Figure V1.** Representative normalized free-surface response-magnitude fields for the N=8 fixed circular-cylinder ring array at T = 0.98, 1.00, 1.02, and 1.40 s. The plotted quantity is the incident-amplitude-normalized frequency-domain free-surface response magnitude. These fields are used as representative spatial visualizations and are not interpreted as confirmed modal or resonance patterns.

**Optional Figure V2.** Array-size comparison of normalized free-surface response-magnitude fields for N=4, 6, and 8 at T = 1.00 s. This figure is optional and should only be used if the corresponding field_abs_N4_Tp100.png, field_abs_N6_Tp100.png, and field_abs_N8_Tp100.png are inserted into the manuscript.
